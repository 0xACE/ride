:GoTo aexpGoTo Statement

A :GoTo statement is a direct alternative to → (branch) and causes execution to jump to the line specified by the first element of aexp. 

The following are equivalent.  See Language Reference: BranchBranch for further details.

      →Exit
      :GoTo Exit
 
      →(N<I←I+1)/End
      :GoTo (N<I←I+1)/End
 
      →1+⎕LC
      :GoTo 1+⎕LC
 
      →10
      :GoTo 10
